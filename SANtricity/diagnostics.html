Important: Perform the following actions only under the guidance of your Technical Support Engineer.
Collect and send my storage array diagnostics
Collect Support Data
Manually collect and send support data to your Technical Support Engineer to use in troubleshooting and problem analysis.
Retrieve Recovery Support Files
Manually download individual recovery support files for sending to your Technical Support Engineer for analysis.
Retrieve Trace Buffers
Retrieve trace buffers to save trace information to a compressed file suitable for sending to your Technical Support Engineer for analysis.
Collect Drive Data
Collect drive data to gather statistical information from all the drives on your storage array.
Collect I/O Path Statistics
Collect I/O path statistics to assist your Technical Support Engineer in analyzing and solving potential performance issues.
Retrieve Health Image
Retrieve a storage array health image to aid your Technical Support Engineer in diagnosing any potential problems.
View and/or take action on my current storage array status
View / Clear Unreadable Sectors
Access unreadable sectors to view a detailed list of all known unreadable sectors on your storage array.
View iSCSI Statistics Packages
View the various types of iSCSI statistics packages available on your storage array.
View / End iSCSI Sessions
View and/or end iSCSI sessions to force initiators off your storage array.
Re-enable Drive Ports
Re-enable any disabled drive ports caused by an improper cable connection (miswire) or a SAS discovery problem.
Clear Recovery Mode
Clear the storage array's recovery mode state and reset the on-board backup to the current storage array configuration.
